    The course starts by outlining the most important factors that need to be considered when preparing for a project, giving you a powerful checklist for your future project planning. You will then learn about the various phases of the project life cycle and of the system development life cycle. This will include overviews of the analysis, planning, design and evaluation phases. Finally, the course presents a project management case study where you can see the various phases of project management being carried out.
